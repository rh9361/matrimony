How it works

  • You can get 5 contacts per week directly whichever you like.
  • After that you can search profiles based on your interest and send notification by clicking send interest
  • Another candidate will check his/her profile and if he/she likes yours then respond by cliking interested or not interested
  • If second candidate is also interested then contacts will be shared.
  • If second candidate is not interested then contacts will not be shared.
  • Please update your profile after few days periodically, because all profiles list is displayed based on latest updated time.
  • updating periodically will increases chances of more visibility
